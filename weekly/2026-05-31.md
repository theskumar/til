# Week of May 31, 2026

Things I learned during the week ending May 31, 2026.

- [Dynamic workflows](https://claude.com/blog/introducing-dynamic-workflows-in-claude-code) in Claude Code. Claude dynamically writes orchestration scripts that spawn tens to hundreds of parallel subagents. It plans, decomposes into subtasks, fans out agents, and checks results before returning a coordinated answer. Adversarial agents try to refute findings, iterating until convergence. Enable via `ultracode` setting or ask Claude to "create a workflow". Notable: Jarred Sumner used it to port Bun from Zig to Rust (~750K lines, 11 days). Available on Max, Team, Enterprise plans. Consumes significantly more tokens than typical sessions.
